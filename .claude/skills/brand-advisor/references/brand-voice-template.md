**Writing rules:**
- Use "you" not "our users"
- Use simple words: "use" not "utilize", "help" not "facilitate"
- Active voice > passive: "We remember" not "It is remembered"
- Specificity > vagueness: numbers, examples, concrete outcomes
- Short sentences default. Long sentences for complex ideas only.

**Forbidden words/phrases:**
- [Word 1] (use [alternative] instead)
- [Word 2]
- "Leverage" (use "use")
- "Innovative" / "cutting-edge" / "revolutionary" (show, don't claim)

## 5. Channel-Specific Adaptations

### LinkedIn (founder/professional)
- Longer form, insight-driven
- First-person, personal experience
- Data and specific numbers

### Twitter/X (tech community)
- Short, punchy, opinionated
- Threads for depth
- Engage with replies

### Product UI
- Ultra-clear, zero jargon
- Outcome-oriented copy
- Calm on errors, specific on success
