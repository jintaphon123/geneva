# Investor Update Template

Monthly update. Send even when things are hard — especially when things are hard.
Investors who hear bad news from you first trust you. Investors who hear it last don't.

---

## Template

```
Subject: [Company] — [Month Year] Update

Hi [Name / all],

[One sentence: overall trajectory — up/flat/down, and the key driver]

---

HIGHLIGHTS
• [Win 1 — specific and measurable]
• [Win 2 — specific and measurable]
• [Win 3 if there is one]

LOWLIGHTS
• [Problem 1 — what happened, what you're doing about it]
• [Problem 2 if relevant]

KEY METRICS
| Metric | This Month | Last Month | Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Metric 1] | [X] | [Y] | [Z] |
| [Metric 2] | [X] | [Y] | [Z] |
| [Metric 3] | [X] | [Y] | [Z] |

WHAT'S NEXT
• [Priority 1 for next month]
• [Priority 2]

ASK
[One specific ask — intro to X, advice on Y, connection to Z]
If nothing needed: "No specific ask this month — just keeping you in the loop."

[Your name]
```

---

## Rules for Investor Updates

**Frequency:** Monthly minimum. Weekly if actively fundraising.

**Length:** Under 300 words for the email body. Metrics table is separate.

**Bad news format:** Problem → What happened → What you're doing → What you need.
Never: Problem → excuse → excuse → excuse.

**The ask:** One specific ask per update. Multiple asks = no asks (they'll pick none).
Make the ask easy to fulfill — "intro to [specific person at specific company]" beats "introductions to VCs."

**Metrics to include:**
- The one metric that best represents momentum (users, revenue, retention)
- Burn / runway if relevant to the investor relationship
- Never include a metric that looks good but isn't real progress

**What NOT to include:**
- Jargon or buzzwords
- Long explanations of things that didn't happen
- Multiple asks
- Apologies for how late the update is (just send it)

---

## Tone Calibration

| Situation | Tone |
|---|---|
| Things going well | Matter-of-fact, not celebratory. Show the data. |
| Things going poorly | Direct, own it, show the plan. No spin. |
| Major pivot | Extra context — explain the decision and the new direction clearly. |
| Fundraising mode | More frequent updates. More specific asks. |
