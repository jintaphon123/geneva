## OKR Writing Rules

**Objectives must:**
- Be inspiring and directional (qualitative)
- Be achievable in one quarter
- Make you slightly uncomfortable — if it's easy, it's not an OKR, it's a task

**Key Results must:**
- Be measurable (number, %, date, binary yes/no)
- Be outcomes, not outputs ("50 users come back weekly" not "build notification feature")
- Have a clear target at start of quarter
- Be things you don't control 100% — otherwise it's just a task

**Confidence scoring:**
- 100% = already done or certain to happen = wrong KR (too easy)
- 70% = right level of stretch
- < 40% = either wrong KR, wrong quarter, or needs unblocking now
- Watch for confidence going down mid-quarter — flag immediately

**Grading at quarter end:**
- ≥ 70%: Good quarter
- 100%: Goal was too easy
- < 50%: Something went wrong — post-mortem needed
