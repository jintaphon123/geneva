# Email Sequence Template

5-email scaffold. Works for welcome/onboarding sequences. Adapt timing and angles for other sequence types.

---

## Sequence Overview

```
Sequence: [name]
Trigger: [what starts it — signup, download, purchase, manual add]
Goal: [primary conversion — activate / purchase / book call / re-engage]
Length: 5 emails over [X] days
Exit: [convert to goal / unsubscribe / sequence complete]
```

---

## Email 1 — Deliver the Promise (Day 0, immediate)

```
Subject: [What they signed up for — deliver it now]
Preview: [Adds context or urgency to subject]

[First name],

[Deliver the exact value they signed up for — no preamble, no "thanks for signing up"]

[One piece of next-step guidance — what should they do first?]

[CTA: single action — "Click here to get started" / "Open the guide" / "Try this now"]

[Your name]
```

**Goal:** First impression. Show that you deliver immediately.
**Avoid:** Thank you pages, "welcome to the community" warmth without substance.

---

## Email 2 — Teach One Thing (Day 2-3)

```
Subject: [The one thing that makes [users] successful]
Preview: [Specific enough to make them open it]

[First name],

[One non-obvious insight or tactic — not the obvious first step they already know]

[Example or proof that this works — specific, not generic]

[CTA: try this specific thing right now]

[Your name]
```

**Goal:** Build expertise. Make them better at the thing they're trying to do.
**Avoid:** Feature list. Stay on one idea.

---

## Email 3 — Remove the Blocker (Day 5-7)

```
Subject: [The #1 reason people don't [achieve outcome] with [product]]
Preview: [Acknowledge the real friction]

[First name],

[Name the #1 objection or friction head-on — don't dance around it]

[Resolve it directly: here's why it's not a problem / here's how others solved it]

[CTA: the specific action that addresses this blocker]

[Your name]
```

**Goal:** Pre-empt churn by addressing the thing that stops people from succeeding.
**Avoid:** Defensive tone. Be direct and honest about the friction.

---

## Email 4 — Social Proof (Day 9-10)

```
Subject: [How [specific type of person] got [specific result]]
Preview: [Specific number or detail — "3x faster" / "in 2 weeks"]

[First name],

[Short story: who they were before → what they did → what happened after. < 150 words]

"[Direct quote if available]" — [Name, role/company if available]

[CTA: do what they did — specific next action]

[Your name]
```

**Goal:** Validation. Social proof that people like them succeed.
**Avoid:** Vague testimonials ("It's great!"). Specificity is everything.

---

## Email 5 — Next Milestone (Day 13-14)

```
Subject: [The next level — what [users] do after they [initial goal]]
Preview: [Tease what's possible beyond the starting point]

[First name],

[Acknowledge they've had time to get started]

[Paint the picture of what's next — the next goal, the next outcome]

[Offer: what's the next thing they should try / the upgrade / the deeper engagement?]

[CTA: specific next step toward the bigger goal]

[Your name]
```

**Goal:** Push to the next conversion milestone (upgrade, referral, deeper feature).
**Avoid:** Hard sell. Frame as "you're ready for the next step."

---

## Cold Outreach Sequence Template (5 touches)

```
Email 1 (Day 1):   First touch — specific observation + one connection + low-friction ask
Email 2 (Day 4):   Different angle — new value point, no mention of email 1 being ignored
Email 3 (Day 9):   Social proof — "Company X was in a similar situation and..."
Email 4 (Day 16):  Address objection — "If timing is the issue..." / "If [common blocker]..."
Email 5 (Day 25):  Breakup — "I'll stop reaching out. If [situation changes], [how to reach me]."
```

**Subject line progression:**
1. First touch: specific trigger-based subject
2. Follow-up 2: different value framing
3. Follow-up 3: case study style
4. Follow-up 4: objection framing
5. Breakup: "[First name] — closing your file"

---

## Metrics to Track

| Metric | Benchmark | Action if below |
|---|---|---|
| Open rate | 25-40% (nurture) / 40-60% (cold) | Fix subject lines |
| Click rate | 2-5% (nurture) / 1-3% (cold) | Fix body + CTA |
| Reply rate | 0.5-3% (cold) | Fix tone + specificity |
| Unsubscribe rate | < 0.5% | Too frequent or wrong audience |
| Conversion rate | Varies by goal | Fix sequence logic or offer |
